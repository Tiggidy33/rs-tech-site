# Product Requirements Document: R & S Technologies IT Support Services

## 1. Overview
R & S Technologies is a dedicated IT support service provider committed to empowering businesses and individual users through personalized, proactive, and transparent technology solutions.  
Motto: “Empowering Your Digital Journey: From Setup to Success.”

Services:
- troubleshooting
- security management
- software and hardware support
- user training
- vendor coordination

Focus: small businesses and home PC users, responsive, flexible, tailor-made support.

## 2. Objectives and Success Criteria

Goals:
- reliable, efficient technical support reducing downtime
- strong security and data protection practices
- personalized remote or on-site options
- user education for better tech management
- transparent pricing and service delivery

Success Measures:
- client satisfaction ratings, repeat engagements
- average response/resolution times
- reduction in incidents from proactive maintenance/training
- growth in managed services subscriptions
- clear, consistent documentation and reporting

## 3. Key Features and Requirements

1. Technical Support and Troubleshooting
- help desk: phone/email/remote tool user requests
- troubleshooting: software/hardware/connectivity fixes, escalation protocols

2. Security and Data Protection
- monitoring/managing firewalls, antivirus, intrusion detection
- role-based user access control

3. Software and Hardware Management
- installation, updates, patch management, compatibility checks
- hardware procurement, installation, repair assistance

4. Documentation and Reporting
- system/user documentation for knowledge sharing
- regular reports on system health, security events, tickets

5. User Training and Education
- onboarding and initial training
- ongoing sessions for efficiency and cybersecurity awareness

6. Vendor and Asset Management
- coordinate provider contracts for service levels
- track inventory and responsible disposal of old equipment

7. Collaboration Tools Administration
- manage Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace for communication and security

### User Scenario
Maria, small business owner, calls because an employee’s laptop cannot connect. prompt remote diagnosis finds corrupted network driver; update restores access within an hour. later, schedules security awareness training.

## 4. Constraints and Assumptions
- on-site availability varies, remote beyond 45 miles may be constrained
- client backup cooperation required; not liable for data loss
- compatibility assumed only with procurement consulting
- legacy systems may not support latest updates
- pricing may adjust based on complexity

## 5. Dependencies and Stakeholders
Dependencies:
- internal teams: support, project management, customer success
- clients: small businesses, home users
- vendors/providers: hardware/software/cloud partners
- regulatory bodies: data protection, e-waste rules

Stakeholders:
- business leadership
- technical staff
- clients

## 6. Timeline and Milestones
- initial consultation/assessment: within 1 week
- hardware/software setup: 1–3 days
- training sessions: quarterly or as requested
- monthly reporting: business clients

## 7. Conclusion
R & S Technologies provides trusted partnership for small businesses and home users with full-cycle IT support: setup, troubleshooting, optimization, education.  
Focus on transparency, proactive management, and client empowerment to minimize disruption and maximize technology value.  
Next steps: finalize service agreements, schedule consultations, align resources, onboard clients quickly.
