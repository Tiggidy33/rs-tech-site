Built around practical support and real follow-through

R & S Technologies exists to make technology feel more manageable, more reliable, and easier to understand for the people who depend on it.

Our Mission

R & S Technologies is committed to empowering businesses and individual users through personalized, proactive, and transparent technology solutions.

We want people to feel supported from setup through troubleshooting, optimization, and everyday use.

Our Values

  • Reliable support that reduces downtime and disruption.
  • Strong attention to security and responsible technology use.
  • Clear communication before, during, and after support work.
  • Service tailored to the customer, not a one-size-fits-all script.
  • User education that helps people become more confident over time.

Team and Experience

We focus on support for small businesses and home users, with hands-on help across troubleshooting, security, software and hardware support, and collaboration tools.

Our approach is grounded in practical service: diagnose the issue, explain the next steps, and work toward a stable outcome.

Local and Customer-First

We serve customers in Hampton Roads and Williamsburg with responsive support, flexible remote and on-site options, and a clear commitment to minimizing disruption.

When you reach out, you get real support centered on listening carefully, communicating honestly, and following through.
