Our Services

## Introduction
At R & S Technologies, we provide comprehensive IT support tailored for small businesses and home users. Our services are designed to ensure reliability, security, and efficiency in your technology use.

## Services Details

### Help Desk Support
- **Description**: Phone and email support for user requests.
- **What Customers Get**: Prompt assistance and guidance for IT-related questions.
- **Common Use Cases**: Answering inquiries, providing basic troubleshooting steps.

### Troubleshooting
- **Description**: Diagnosis and resolution of software, hardware, and connectivity issues.
- **What Customers Get**: Quick fixes to restore functionality and minimize downtime.
- **Common Use Cases**: Resolving corrupted drivers, fixing network connectivity problems, addressing software errors.

### Security Support
- **Description**: Monitoring and managing firewalls, antivirus software, and intrusion detection systems.
- **What Customers Get**: Enhanced data protection, role-based access control, and proactive threat prevention.
- **Common Use Cases**: Preventing cyber attacks, securing sensitive information, implementing security policies.

### Software and Hardware Support
- **Description**: Installation, updates, patch management, and compatibility checks for software and hardware.
- **What Customers Get**: Optimized systems, repair assistance, and procurement guidance.
- **Common Use Cases**: Setting up new hardware, applying software updates, ensuring compatibility with existing systems.

### User Training
- **Description**: Onboarding sessions and ongoing training for efficiency and cybersecurity awareness.
- **What Customers Get**: Improved tech management skills and knowledge to handle common issues independently.
- **Common Use Cases**: Training employees on new tools, conducting cybersecurity awareness workshops, providing onboarding for new users.

### Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace Help
- **Description**: Administration and management of collaboration tools for communication and security.
- **What Customers Get**: Properly configured and secure productivity suites.
- **Common Use Cases**: Setting up email accounts, configuring security settings, managing user permissions.
