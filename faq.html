What hours do you provide support?

We provide support during business hours, Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM. Emergency support may be available outside these hours on a case-by-case basis.

Do you offer remote and on-site help?

Yes, we offer both remote support via phone, email, and remote tools, and on-site assistance where feasible. On-site availability may vary, and remote support is preferred for locations beyond 45 miles.

Which platforms do you support?

We support a wide range of platforms including Windows, macOS, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and various software and hardware configurations. Compatibility is assumed for systems we assist with procurement.

How quickly can I expect a response?

We aim for initial consultation within 1 week, and hardware/software setup within 1-3 days. Response times depend on the complexity of the issue.

Do you provide training?

Yes, we offer user training including onboarding and ongoing sessions for efficiency and cybersecurity awareness.
